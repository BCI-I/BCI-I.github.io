What

We're hosting the first MIT hackathon on Brain Computer Interfaces.


The hackathon will be hosted at MIT in February 2024. It will be a 48-hour event where teams of 3-5 MIT members will compete to devise the best algorithm to control a robot using only their brain. The hackathon will be open to all MIT members and will be free to attend - winning teams will be awarded a cash prize. We will provide all the necessary hardware and software for the hackathon, you just need to bring your laptop and your brain!

Why i

Brain Computer Interfaces (BCIs) enable direct control of computers and other devices using only brain activity. They are a rapidly growing field of research with applications in medicine, entertainment, and more and promise to revolutionize the way we interact with technology improve the lives of people suffering from neurological disorders & injuries. We want to spark interest in BCI & encourage research in this field by hosting a hackathon.
