X.on EEG headset

The X.on EEG headsets by Brain Products is caracterized by its flexibility, ease of use and high quality signal. It features 7 electrodes (passive sponge-based; channels: F3, F4, C3, Cz, C4, P3, P4 + ear clip ground) weighs only 110g, allows for wireless data streaming over Bluetooth and has more than 5 hours of battery life.
