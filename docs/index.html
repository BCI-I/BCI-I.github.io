Our Mission

Welcome to the Brain Computer Interfaces Initiative (BCI-I), an organization dedicated to advancing education and research on BCIs worldwide.


Our goal is to actively promote the exchange of knowledge surrounding BCIs, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to explore this dynamic field and engage in discussions about its potential and associated risks.


Committed to fostering an inclusive and equitable future, we strive to make BCI technology accessible to all, irrespective of background or expertise.


We aspire to ignite curiosity in brilliant minds globally, encouraging them to explore and pursue careers in BCI research or embark on entrepreneurial ventures within the realm of brain-computer interfaces.


Our Plan

The BCI-I envisions the creation of local chapters closely linked to universities and industry leaders in both developed and developing countries.


Through the establishment of a robust network of local chapters, our objective is to facilitate the exchange of knowledge and resources among researchers and students worldwide.


Collaborating closely with research institutions and private companies, we strive to expand opportunities for students in developing countries, allowing them to pursue careers in BCI research.
