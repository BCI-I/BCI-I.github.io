Our Mission

BCI-I aims to promote education and research on BCI across the globe.

We aim to form a network of local Chapters, merging close ties to local communities with the reach of a global organization.

We want to work closely with industry leaders to inspire the next generation of BCI researchers and developers.

We want to make knowledge about BCI accessible to everyone
